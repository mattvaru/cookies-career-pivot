end of an era.

After 3.5 years, 1 million CMS items, a few chaotic rebrands, and more “quick syncs” than humanly necessary, I’m officially signing off.

But before I go, I made one last Webflow site — because obviously.

This is my (playfully coded) love letter to the Webflow community, teammates, and projects along the way. From spreadsheets that defied logic to Slack threads that deserved Emmys, thank you for making this era unforgettable.

To my Team

Thank you for being the kind of teammates who made this more than just work.

For every wild idea you said yes to, every late-night doc you helped shape, every time you chose curiosity over ego — I’m endlessly grateful. You taught me how to lead with heart,build with purpose, and laugh through the chaos.

I came to Webflow to grow a community. I left with one.
